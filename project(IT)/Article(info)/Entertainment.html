Computers now have a major impact on the entertainment industry. They are employed to create striking special effects for movies. They are employed in the editing of movies, the creation of animated features, the creation of multimedia presentations, etc. Computers are used to simulate games and experiment with different gaming strategies. For entertainment, Computers are widely used for viewing films and television shows online, creating Cartoon animation, playing video games, and using computer applications for picture and video editing. Entertainment material can be stored using ICT products like compact discs and memory cards for future use. ICT has improved the quality of entertainment and leisure activities by developing better graphics for music and movies.

  • Movies

    • With the advancement of video cards and internet speed, movies are now at a click of the mouse button
    • Just think of the Baahubali movie, what great VFX etc. was used. Movies now contain numerous visual tricks and treats that could not be fulfilled without the aid of computers.


  • Music

    • Any musical composition that we listen to these days is authentic while the piece of the song is composed, while it's miles performed, or while it's miles reproduced. Most songs have a few kinds of electronic instrumentation, which is identical to the case of songs in TV commercials, cartoons, and famous movies.
    • Many song composition languages resemble programming languages. Today virtual audios are extensively used.


  • Art

    • Since the stone age, Art has developed with humankind. The devices and the medium have changed however the capability of art continues as before. Computer Art can be categorized into two significant categories - Computer graphics and image processing. Computer graphics are utilized in Computer Animation. People involved in computer graphics need programming knowledge as well as an understanding of perspective, shading, hidden-line, hidden-surface removal, shadowing, ray tracing, etc. in image creation. The intuitive art given by the Computer permits one to be the chief and to encounter the work in his own "time".


    • Gaming

      • The Computer gaming industry has today increased more than the world of music and film ventures. The impact of computer games on the young people of today is similar to that of the social impact of music, political developments, and even religion on the youth culture of the past. Of late, Computer games have been drawing in individuals of assorted types into utilizing Computers.
